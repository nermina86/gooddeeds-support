About GoodDeeds

GoodDeeds helps you build a daily habit of kindness. Complete a few simple good deeds each day and track your streak.

FAQ

Can I decline deeds? Yes. You can snooze the deeds you are not comfortable of doing

How many deeds per day? You can choose your preferred desired count in Settings.

Can I create custom list? Yes. You can personalise the app to add your own good deeds.

Sync with Apple Watch? Yes — the app syncs your daily deeds between iPhone and Apple Watch.

Data & privacy? We store minimal data and don’t collect any personal information. See Privacy Policy below.
